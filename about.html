Our Story

Founded in 2020 during a pivotal moment in healthcare history, Manoi emerged from a simple yet powerful realization: traditional medical education wasn't adequately preparing students for the complexities of modern healthcare practice.

Our founders, experienced healthcare professionals and educators, recognized the critical gap between classroom learning and clinical application. They envisioned a platform that would revolutionize healthcare education by combining cutting-edge technology with hands-on clinical experience.

Today, Manoi has grown into a trusted partner for over 5,000 students and 200+ healthcare institutions worldwide, consistently achieving a 95% job placement rate for our graduates. We continue to innovate while staying true to our founding principles of excellence, accessibility, and real-world relevance.
